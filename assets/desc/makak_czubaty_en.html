Crested Macaque
Macaca nigra
Order: Primates
Family: Old World Monkey
Characteristics
Black, longish fur, vestigial tail, black face, the young pink, pale pink gluteal calluses.
Lifestyle
It lives in pairs or small groups, mostly on the ground, juveniles and females with children protect themselves in the trees.
Breeding
Pregnancy lasts about 5 months.
Nesting site
It is an endemic species occurring only on two islands of Indonesia Sulawesi (formerly known as Celebes) - to the north of the island, and the island of Pulau Bacan.
Protection
Threats: Poaching.
Interesting facts
  • By the inhabitants of the island Celebes crested macaque is considered a sacred animal.
