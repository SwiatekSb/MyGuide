Agouti Gold
Dasyprocta leporina
Order: Rodents
Family: agutiowate
Characteristics
The length of the rodent reaches 50 cm, the tail is very short (about 2 cm).
Lifestyle
It lives singly or in pairs, very rarely herds.
Breeding
I do not have a clear breeding season.
Nesting site
Forests, savanna, fields.
Protection
Threats: There is a pest in its homeland.
Interesting facts
  • The first mention of agouti can be found in the book Andre theret, the sixteenth-century French cosmographer.
