Lycaon
Lycaon pictus
Order: prey
Family: canines
Characteristics
It is an animal of a harmonious build, with large, broad, rounded ears, short tapered snout to the end.
Lifestyle
Wild dogs are mainly diurnal.
Breeding
Due to the fact that the herd is strong hierarchy, breeding join only dominant individuals.
Nesting site
Savannah grasslands, savannas, woody, semi-deserts, acacia scrub, bush, mountainous areas.
Protection
Threats: Loss of natural areas to live, and competition in the form of lions and hyenas spotted that feed on the same grounds.
Interesting facts
