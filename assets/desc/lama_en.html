Lama
Lama glama
Order: artiodactyls
Family: camelids
Characteristics
Body length is about 120 cm, tail - 15 cm, height at the withers - 102-119 cm, weight - 113-250 kg (weight at birth - 8-18 kg).
Lifestyle
They feed on grass, leaves, shrubs and trees.
Breeding
They breed throughout the year.
Nesting site
Mountains.
Protection
Threats: They are killed for their fur and wool.
Interesting facts
  • The name Lama glama was introduced by Linneus in 1758.
