Chapman's zebra
Equus burchelli chapmanni
Order: Equidae
Family: equines
Characteristics
Subspecies of the plains zebra.
Lifestyle
Zebras live in herds composed of females (mares) and young (foals), and leads them to a male (stallion).
Breeding
The largest number of births is recorded during the rainy season.
Nesting site
Savanna
