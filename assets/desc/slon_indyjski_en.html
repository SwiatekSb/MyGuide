Indian elephant
Elephas maximus
Order: proboscidean
Family: elephantine
Characteristics
Body length ranges from 5.5 m to 6.5 m, height at the withers from 2.5 m to 3 m, weight of 2 700 kg to 5 500 kg.
Lifestyle
Elephants live in herds of family, counting 15 - 30 individuals, led by an old female.
Breeding
Pregnancy lasts 19-21 months.
Nesting site
Central Africa.
Protection
Threats: Threatened with extinction.
Interesting facts
  • To taste these great mammals are swimming in rivers, during which the use of trumpets to pour the water.
