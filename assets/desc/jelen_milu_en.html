Milu deer
Elaphurus davidianus
Order: artiodactyls
Family: deer
Characteristics
Reddish coloration summer, winter-brown.
Lifestyle
They are herd animals.
Breeding
The female gives birth in April or May one to two young.
Nesting site
Only in zoos.
Protection
Threats: critically endangered species with extinction.
Interesting facts
  • Exterminated around the third century, N. E.
