Mangabe Rudoczelna
Cercocebus torquatus
Order: Primates
Family: Old World Monkey
Characteristics
Reaches a length of 100-150 cm (including tail 50-80 cm) and weighs 15 kg.
Lifestyle
These monkeys as happy staying both in the trees and on the ground.
Breeding
Pregnancy lasts about 24 weeks, after which the female gives birth to one young.
Nesting site
Coastal rainforest.
Protection
Threats: Its extinction may contribute to deforestation in Africa.
Interesting facts
  • In Africa, often referred to as monkeys czterookimi because of snow-white eyes, which resemble a second pair of eyes.
