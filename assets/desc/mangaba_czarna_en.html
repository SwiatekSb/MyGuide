Mangabe Black
Lophocebus aterrimus
Order: Primates
Family: Old World Monkey
Characteristics
Slender body, body length 70 cm, tail up to 80 cm, body weight over 15 kg.
Lifestyle
They have a habit of mutual care for the cleanliness of the body.
Breeding
Gestation lasts about 170 days, the female gives birth to usually one young.
Nesting site
Forests of Central Africa.
Protection
Threats: Its extinction may contribute to deforestation in Africa.
