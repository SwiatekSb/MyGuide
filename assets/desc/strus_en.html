Ostrich
Struthio camelus
Order: Ostriches
Family: Ostriches
Characteristics
The biggest and fastest (runs up to 70km / h) modern flightless bird.
Lifestyle
Normally live in herds of 5-6 individuals (one male with a harem), in the company of other animal species.
Breeding
The male prepares the nest alone, wydrapuje cavity in the ground.
Nesting site
Desert, semi-desert and savanna.
Protection
Interesting facts
  • Ostrich egg is the largest among animals living today!
