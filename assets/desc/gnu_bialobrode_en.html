Gnu bialobrode
Connochaetes taurinus albojubatus
Order: artiodactyls
Family: bovinae
Characteristics
Subspecies wildebeest brindle, white coloration of the hair, beard and chaps.
Lifestyle
Antelope living in large flocks, often with other species of animals.
Breeding
The dominant bulls defend access to a harem consisting of females with whom they mate.
Nesting site
Savanna
Protection
Threats: Massive hunting and reduction of habitat due to human activities.
