Rhesus Macaque
Macaca mulatta
Order: Primates
Family: Old World Monkey
Characteristics
His body reaches a length of 45-64 cm, 19-32 cm tail, body weight male in the range 6-12 kg.
Lifestyle
They are social animals, but do not show territorialism, their herds can count to two hundred individuals, but are generally smaller.
Breeding
The litter is born mostly one monkey with a birth weight of 400-500 g Young remain with their mother until they reach independence.
Nesting site
Dry and open areas, meadows, forests, mountain peaks of 2,500 meters above sea level to
Protection
Threats: There is danger of extinction.
