Capybara
Hydrochoerus hydrochaeris
Order: Rodents
Family: marowate
Characteristics
It is the largest of living rodents today.
Lifestyle
These land-water animals and lead a life in the open.
Breeding
The female reaches sexual maturity around the age of 18 months.
Nesting site
The banks of rivers and marshy areas.
Protection
Threats: They are killed for their meat and skins.
Interesting facts
  • Are bred as pets.
