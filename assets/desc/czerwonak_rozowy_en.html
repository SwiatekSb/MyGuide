Flamingo pink
Phoenicopterus ruber
Order: flamingos
Family: Flamingos
Characteristics
Lack of a clear sexual dimorphism.
Lifestyle
They feed in shallow backwaters.
Breeding
During the year draws one brood, consisting in March - April one (rarely two) egg.
Nesting site
Salt lakes, lagoons, estuaries.
Protection
Threats: The future is very uncertain.
Interesting facts
  • The classification of this family is debatable.
