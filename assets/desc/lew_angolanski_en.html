Leo Angolan
Panthera leo bleyenberghi
Order: prey
Family: felines
Characteristics
The largest African predator.
Lifestyle
The only species of big cat that lives in the herd and creates a specific social group.
Breeding
Females tend to be synchronized oestrus, which allows at a later time to care for kittens also from other females.
Nesting site
Savanna
Protection
Threats: stalking, hunting, organized by residents because of increasing conflict with humans, poaching for obtaining trophies and habitat fragmentation.
