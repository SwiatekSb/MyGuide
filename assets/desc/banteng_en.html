Banteng
Bos Javanicus
Order: artiodactyls
Family: bovinae
Characteristics
Reaches 180-200 cm in length, the height at the withers 130-170 cm, weight 500-900 kg (females smaller than males).
Lifestyle
They are active at night and at dusk, extremely skittish, live in herds consisting of one adult male and females with young, of up to 30 animals.
Breeding
Gestation lasts 285-300 days, the female gives birth to one young, which matures at the age of 2 years.
Nesting site
Mountain areas, wetlands and dry.
Protection
Threats: The population of this mammal is still threatened.
Interesting facts
  • Banteng very difficult to tame.
